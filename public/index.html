FAQ

Common questions

How does it work? +
Upload one clear, front-facing photo of yourself. Our AI analyzes your facial features and generates professional headshots across multiple styles — different outfits, backgrounds, and lighting setups. The results look like you hired a professional photographer.
How long does it take? +
Most orders complete in 3-8 minutes depending on the package size. You can check the status in real-time on your results page.
What kind of photo should I upload? +
A clear, front-facing photo with good lighting. Natural light works best. Avoid sunglasses, hats, or heavy filters. A smartphone selfie is perfect — no professional camera needed.
Can I use these for LinkedIn/business? +
Absolutely. All packages include personal and professional use rights. The Executive package includes a full commercial license for business websites, marketing, and advertising.
Is my photo stored or shared? +
Your uploaded photo is used only to generate your headshots and is automatically deleted within 24 hours. We never share, sell, or use your images for training.
What if I'm not satisfied? +
We offer a 100% money-back guarantee. If you're not happy with your headshots, contact us and we'll refund your purchase — no questions asked.
